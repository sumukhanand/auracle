How to use: Take a free hearing test at hearingtest.online or mimi.io wearing your headphones at your normal listening volume. Enter the dB threshold values below — positive numbers mean you need more volume at that frequency. We invert and scale them into parametric EQ filter settings.

Tip: Use the scale slider to reduce the correction intensity if the result sounds over-processed.
